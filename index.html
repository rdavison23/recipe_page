Misir Wot

Misir Wot — also spelled Misir Wat — is a beloved Ethiopian
dish made from red lentils simmered in a richly spiced sauce. It's a staple in
Ethiopian cuisine, especiall y popular among vegetarians and vegans due to its
hearty flavor and protein-packed profile.

Ingredients

  • 1large yellow onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 3 Roma tomato, diced
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 2 tbsp berbere spice
  • 1 cup red lentils
  • 2.5 cups vegetable or chicken broth
  • 1 tsp salt
